## 🔍 Key Features & Deep-Dives

### 1. Dual-Axis Monthly Trend Analysis
* **Visualization:** Interactive dual-axis line chart mapping **Sales** (light blue) against **Customer Count** (dark blue) over an annual cycle.
* **Operational Value:** Helps identify customer buying behavior patterns and seasonal demand spikes (e.g., Q4 peak performance vs. Q1 historical lows).

### 2. Market Segmentation & Product Performance
* **Geographic Distribution:** A horizontal bar chart tracking the **Number of Customers in Cities**, highlighting primary revenue clusters (e.g., New York City, Los Angeles, Philadelphia, San Francisco, and Seattle).
* **Category Popularity:** Analyzes product inventory demand, establishing **Office Supplies** as the highest volume category, followed by **Furniture** and **Technology**.
* **Transactional Ledger:** A structured data table tracking line-item performance down to the exact date, sub-category, and localized daily transaction value.

### 3. Dynamic Slicers & Interactivity
* **Temporal Filters:** Toggle views across individual fiscal years (**2014**, **2015**, and **2016**).
* **Geographic Slicers:** An extensive, searchable checklist to isolate individual regional markets from Aberdeen to Arlington Heights.
